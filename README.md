# Dataprism

> [Open via Browser](http://checkyourdata.cu.ma/)
> [Install onto your iDevice](test)
## 🌐 About the Project

This app is more than just a tool — it’s a window into your digital self.  
Every time you visit a website, your device shares information. Most of it happens silently.

This project makes that visible.

---

### ✨ What it does
- 📱 Shows what your device reveals to websites  
- 🌍 Displays data like IP, location, device, browser, and network  
- 🔐 Highlights permissions (camera, mic, location, etc.)  
- 💾 Visualizes storage (cookies, local/session storage)  
- ⚡ Updates data in real time where possible  

---

### 🎯 Why it exists

Instead of overwhelming you with technical details, the app focuses on clarity.  
You don’t just see data — you understand it.

- 🧠 Turns complex data into simple insights  
- 👁️ Makes the invisible visible  
- 🎛️ Helps you feel more in control of your digital presence  

---

### 🍏 Experience

- Minimal, Apple-inspired design  
- Smooth animations & clean layout  
- Dark & light mode support  
- Feels intuitive and personal  

---

### 🔒 Privacy First

- 🚫 No tracking  
- 📴 Works fully on-device  
- 🔍 No backend, no data collection
